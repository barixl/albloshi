Serving the Kingdom's Critical Industries with Unwavering Strength

Mohammad Abdulla Albloshi Trading Co. is an established, highly diversified industrial supplier and trading conglomerate headquartered in Dammam, Saudi Arabia. We operate dynamically across the GCC to guarantee operational continuity, absolute regulatory compliance, and rapid supply execution for key regional operators.

  • Industrial and Building Supplies: Seamless carbon and stainless steel piping materials, high-integrity industrial valves, flanges, structural fasteners, and heavy welding consumables.
  • Intelligent Specialty Chemistry: Regional distributor of TELLABS water treatment coagulants, scale inhibitors, Granular Activated Carbon, and process formulations.
  • Wholesale Food Distribution: Enterprise-scale logistics and wholesale import of premium bulk Basmati rice, double-refined palm oil, and restaurant hospitality packaging.
  • Skilled Manpower Supply: Deploying qualified structural welders, industrial electricians, piping fitters, and shutdown maintenance crews under strictly compliant scopes.
