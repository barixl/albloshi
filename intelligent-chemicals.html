About Tellabs Chemicals

Tellabs Chemicals Pvt. Ltd. is a leading manufacturer and exporter of specialty chemicals focused on delivering innovative, sustainable, and high-performance solutions for industrial applications. With advanced manufacturing facilities, strong R&D capabilities, and a commitment to quality, Tellabs serves industries across water treatment, food processing, sugar, pulp & paper, fuel optimization, and industrial cleaning.
